'Star Wars: The Force Awakens' International Trailer Reveals More Movie Footage

By Cesar R. Bustamante JR., New York Daily News

Friday, November 6th 2015

The galaxy far, far away is looking even darker but not hopeless.

The international trailer of “Star Wars: The Force Awakens” shows more footage from the movie including a dark Kylo Ren with a lightsaber to Rey’s neck.

Taking place 30 years after the events of “Return of the Jedi,” the Republic still terrorizes the galaxy.

This trailer shows new footage from the movie including First Order ships making their menacingly arrival over the horizon. There are also glimpses of Storm Troopers torching a camp and Kylo Ren (Adam Driver) with an outreached hand preparing to use the Force.

But the Resistance aren’t going to give the galaxy up without a fight, as we see Princess Leia and C-3PO gathered with others in the possible Resistance base and Chewbacca blowing up the bridge of an enemy ship after giving his iconic howl.

And by the end of the trailer Rey, played by Daisy Ridley, says, “Hope is not lost today. It is found.”

Despite all the new footage, one thing is still missing: Luke Skywalker, the hero of episodes IV through VI. Mark Hamill is reprising his role for episode VII, but it remains unclear how.

“Star Wars: The Force Awakens” hits American theaters on Dec. 18. It opens two days earlier in Dubai.
